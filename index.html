Who we are

A home for Qur'an, built on understanding

Basira means insight — and that is exactly what we aim to give every student. Our teachers don't just correct pronunciation; they help students understand meaning, build a lasting connection with the Qur'an, and carry good character into daily life.

  • Certified Ijazah-holding teachers, screened and interviewed personally
  • Separate, comfortable classes for sisters with female teachers
  • Flexible scheduling across time zones, 7 days a week
  • Monthly progress reports shared directly with parents

Our Mission

To make authentic, one-to-one Qur'an education accessible to every home, taught with patience, clarity and heart.
